GUESS WHO — HOW TO PLAY
Objective

Guess which character your opponent secretly picked by asking yes/no questions and eliminating suspects. You win when you correctly identify their character before they identify yours.

Steps
1

Pick a character

During the selection phase, choose one character secretly. Only you can see your choice.

2

Ask yes/no questions

On your turn send a yes/no question (e.g., "Does your character wear glasses?"). Your opponent answers honestly.

3

Eliminate cards

If the answer excludes some characters, cross them out on your board. This helps narrow down the possibilities.

4

Make a guess

When you think you know who it is, pick a character and submit a guess. If correct — you win. If wrong — your turn ends.

Tips
  • Start with broad questions that split the board roughly in half.
  • Keep track of both what you asked and what you learned.
  • Use elimination (double-click/cross) to remember excluded characters.
This web version supports 2 players over the network. Use the room system to invite friends.
